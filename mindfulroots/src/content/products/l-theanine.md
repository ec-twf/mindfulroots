L-theanine is the reason a cup of green tea feels calmer than a cup of coffee, despite both containing caffeine. It increases alpha-wave activity — the brain rhythm associated with relaxed, wakeful states — and unlike most calming compounds it does this without making you sleepy.

That "relaxed but alert" quality makes it one of the few stress-support ingredients that's equally at home before a stressful meeting and before bed.

## What to look for

- Plain L-theanine (Suntheanine is a well-known branded form)
- 100–200 mg per serving
- Optional caffeine + theanine combos if focus is your main goal
